Cobblestone and Basalt Generators come in different tiers that yield different results.

**Tiers:**

* Cobblestone – 1 block/sec
* Iron – 8 blocks/sec
* Gold – 16 blocks/sec
* Diamond – 32 blocks/sec
* Netherite – 64 blocks/sec
