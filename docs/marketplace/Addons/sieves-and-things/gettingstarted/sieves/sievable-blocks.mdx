## Sievable Blocks

Many blocks can be *sieved.* Depending on the block and Mesh, you will get different results.

Click a block below to find out what it drops and from what Mesh.

:::info
No matter what Mesh, the block being sieved always drop the same loot. However, the higher the tier of the Mesh, the more likely you are to get better drops

See the loot tables below for each block to find out more.
:::
