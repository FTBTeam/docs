---
title: Timed Quests
sidebar_position: 4
---

Timed Quests include Daily, Weekly, Monthly and Yearly challenges. Each period refreshes on its own timer and is unique to each player.

You can re-roll a timed quest by spending XP levels. The cost increases each time you re-roll and resets when that timer refreshes.

Tip: Use the "Timed Quests" menu to jump straight to each period.
