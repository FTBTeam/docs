---
title: Pinning Quests
sidebar_position: 2
---

Pins let you keep important quests easy to find by moving them to the top of your quest list.

To pin a quest, open any quest you want to track. Inside the quest screen, select the "Pin Quest" button. Once pinned, the button will change to "Unpin Quest".

After pinning a quest, return to the main Quest Book menu. You'll see a new button called "Pinned Quests".

Pinned quests stay there until you unpin them or claim the reward.
