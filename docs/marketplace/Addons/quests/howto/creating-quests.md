sidebar_position: 3

Ready to build your own adventures?
Open the Quest Book in Creative mode and tap "Create Quests".

1. Make a chapter: choose a name, description and icon.
2. Open your chapter and tap "Add Quest" or "Add Random Quest".
3. Pick a task type and fill in the fields.
4. Choose rewards, then submit.

Tips:
-  Random Quest uses the timed quest rules for quick ideas.
- Use the tooltips for quick guidance.
- Custom options let you type your own ids or textures.
- You can edit or remove quests later from the chapter menu.
