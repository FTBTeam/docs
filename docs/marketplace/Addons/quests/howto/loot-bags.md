---
title: Loot Bags
sidebar_position: 2
---

Loot Bags are randomized rewards designed with tiers, the higher the tier, the better the loot.

There are 5 Loot Bags:
-Common Loot Bag
- Uncommon Loot Bag
- Rare Loot Bag
- Epic Loot Bag
- Legendary Loot Bag

To open a Loot Bag, hold it in your main hand and right-click.

If you want to upgrade your loot, you can do so by combining 9 of any bag to obtain the next tier. For example 9 Common Loot Bags will craft an Uncommon Loot Bag, and so on.
